Who is Insculpo, anyways?

I am a game designer who makes games when they are not illustrating or creating a sci-fi setting. I have prior experiences with freelancing and long term group projects including a thesis project for USC Games. The skillset is flexible, for I adapt to the needs of the project and the needs of the client to work towards the best possible project to the best of my abilities. That is the guarantee I provide as a freelancer to any client. On this website you will find the mindgarden I maintain for my setting, a gallery of curated illustrations and examples of prior game projects I have worked on.
